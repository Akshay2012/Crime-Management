About Us

Providing Mumbai with security services for over 20 years Founded in 1947, Mumbai Crime Management has provided security services for over 20 years. Our company was established by: current and retired law enforcement, private citizens and security specialists. Our founding group realized that traditional law enforcement agencies could not effectively safeguard private property in all situations. In most instances, the crime is in process and completed by the time a police officer has arrived. The security industry calls for proactive units rather than reactive units. Should the need arise and with our officers on-site,Crime Management affords our clients the knowledge that their property is protected in real-time.
